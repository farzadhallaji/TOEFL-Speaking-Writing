### Critical Thinking Development


### **Q1: Some people prefer to watch entertainment TV programs; others prefer to watch educational programs. Which do you prefer? Explain why.**
1. "Educational programs foster **critical thinking development** by presenting thought-provoking content that challenges viewers to analyze and reflect."
2. "While entertainment offers relaxation, educational shows enhance **logical reasoning** by encouraging viewers to evaluate real-world issues."
3. "Watching informative content supports **critical thinking abilities**, as it requires interpreting data, forming opinions, and connecting ideas."
