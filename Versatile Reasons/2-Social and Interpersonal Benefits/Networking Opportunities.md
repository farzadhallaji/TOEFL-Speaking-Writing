### Networking Opportunities

### **Q8: Do you agree or disagree that university students should have part-time jobs? Why?**
1. "Part-time jobs provide students with **networking opportunities**, allowing them to connect with professionals who can guide their career paths."
2. "Balancing work with academics teaches students how to **build rapport** with colleagues and managers, strengthening their interpersonal skills."
3. "These experiences not only expand networks but also uncover potential **career opportunities** through practical exposure to industries."

2. "Social activities encourage students to **connect with mentors**, such as faculty advisors or senior members, who can offer guidance and support."
3. "These interactions often lead to long-term **professional connections**, proving invaluable in both academic and career growth."
