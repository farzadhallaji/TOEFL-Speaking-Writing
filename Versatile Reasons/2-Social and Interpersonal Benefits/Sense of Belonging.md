### Sense of Belonging

### **Q15: Do you agree or disagree that all university students should engage in social activities such as joining clubs or sports groups?**
1. "Joining clubs fosters a **sense of belonging**, helping students feel more connected to their university community."
2. "Social activities **promote inclusion** by encouraging students to interact with peers who share similar interests."
3. "These opportunities create a supportive and **inclusive environment**, making the transition to university life smoother."

3. "Short-term separation promotes **community spirit**, as children learn to adapt and engage with different social groups."
