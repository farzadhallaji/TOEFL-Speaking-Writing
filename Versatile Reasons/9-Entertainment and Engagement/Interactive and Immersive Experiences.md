#### **Q30: Do you agree that extra free time at work improves productivity? Why or why not?**
1. "Extra free time provides a much-needed **immersive experience** in personal hobbies, leading to increased creativity and productivity at work."
2. "Short breaks during the day offer **sensory engagement**, helping employees recharge and refocus on tasks."
3. "Allowing free time at work encourages **fully engaging** in tasks afterward, reducing burnout and enhancing efficiency."

---

#### **Q3: Do you prefer recorded music or live performances? Why?**
1. "Live performances provide an **interactive experience**, creating a connection between the audience and the artist that recordings cannot replicate."
2. "Recorded music offers **sensory engagement** with pristine sound quality, making it ideal for personal enjoyment."
3. "Live concerts **capture attention** with their dynamic atmosphere and real-time energy, making them unforgettable."

#### **Q32: Should cities replace parks with housing complexes? Why or why not?**

2. "Replacing parks would eliminate **participatory activities** like jogging or outdoor yoga, crucial for community well-being."
3. "City parks promote **real-time interaction** among residents, fostering a sense of community and shared engagement."

### **Collocations in Context**

- **immersive experience**: "Spending time in nature provides an immersive experience that calms the mind and reduces stress."
- **fully engaging**: "A team-building exercise that is fully engaging can boost morale and productivity."
- **capture attention**: "The stunning fireworks display captured the attention of everyone at the event."
- **interactive platforms**: "Social media platforms have evolved into interactive platforms that connect people worldwide."
- **real-time interaction**: "The app’s real-time interaction feature allows users to collaborate seamlessly."
- **participatory activities**: "Community events with participatory activities like dance workshops create a sense of inclusion."
- **sensory engagement**: "Cooking classes offer sensory engagement by combining hands-on learning with rich aromas and flavors."
