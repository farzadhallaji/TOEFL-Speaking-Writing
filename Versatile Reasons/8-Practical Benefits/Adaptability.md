#### **Q10: Some people prefer working in an office; others prefer working from home. Which do you prefer and why?**
1. "Working from home offers a **flexible arrangement**, allowing me to adapt my schedule to personal and professional needs."  
2. "An office environment encourages **adjusting to circumstances**, fostering teamwork and in-person collaboration that remote work may lack."  
3. "Remote work demonstrates **remarkable adaptability**, making it easier to accommodate diverse responsibilities."

#### **Q18: Some students studying abroad prefer living with roommates from the same cultural background, while others prefer international roommates. Which do you prefer? Explain why.**
1. "Living with international roommates enhances **cultural adaptability**, helping me adjust to global perspectives and new ways of life."  
2. "Roommates from the same cultural background offer a **customized solution** to homesickness, providing comfort in familiar customs."  
3. "Having international roommates fosters a **versatile option** to explore diverse traditions while learning to coexist in a globalized environment."

#### **Q36: Some recent graduates volunteer before starting their careers, while others begin work immediately. Which approach is better and why?**
1. "Volunteering allows graduates to **adapt to change**, preparing them for professional challenges with real-world experience."  
2. "Starting work immediately provides a **flexible solution** to building financial independence while learning on the job."  
3. "Taking time to volunteer demonstrates **remarkable adaptability**, as it nurtures soft skills and provides exposure to various career paths."

#### **Q80: Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? The increasing number of different resources on the Internet has actually made it more difficult to find reliable information.**
1. "The abundance of online resources necessitates a **highly adaptable** approach to discerning credible information from unreliable sources."  
2. "A **customized solution**, such as using verified databases, can make navigating the internet’s vast information landscape more manageable."  
3. "To **accommodate diverse needs**, search engines must evolve to highlight reliable sources while filtering out misinformation."
