### **Q21: Do you prefer buying used or new books? Why?**
1. "Buying used books helps **conserve resources** by reducing the demand for new paper production."
2. "Used books promote **eco-conscious decisions**, ensuring materials are reused and waste is minimized."
3. "While new books support authors directly, opting for second-hand copies aligns with principles of **sustainable alternatives**."
