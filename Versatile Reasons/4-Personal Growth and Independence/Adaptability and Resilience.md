### Adaptability and Resilience



### **Q18: Should students living abroad have roommates from the same or different cultural backgrounds? Why?**
1. "Living with international roommates helps students **develop adaptability**, as they learn to navigate diverse cultural norms and communication styles."
2. "Such experiences foster resilience by teaching individuals how to **adjust to challenges** that arise from differing lifestyles and perspectives."
3. "Building relationships across cultures enables students to **thrive in adversity**, turning potential conflicts into opportunities for personal growth."

3. "Short periods of separation foster resilience by providing opportunities for children to **adjust to challenges** and develop confidence."

2. "Such autonomy fosters **resilience building**, enabling individuals to handle uncertainty and challenges effectively."
3. "While structured environments provide clarity, working independently teaches employees to **embrace flexibility** in dynamic situations."
