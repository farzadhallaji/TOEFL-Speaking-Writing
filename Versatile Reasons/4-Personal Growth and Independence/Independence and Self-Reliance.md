## **Personal Growth and Independence**

### Independence and Self-Reliance


### **Q20: Do you agree or disagree that it is important for a child’s development to spend short periods of time living away from home? Why or why not?**
1. "Living away from home for short periods helps children **build independence**, as they learn to make decisions without immediate parental guidance."
2. "These experiences foster **self-reliance** by encouraging children to manage daily routines and unexpected challenges on their own."
3. "Spending time away enhances **adaptability**, preparing children for transitions in life such as moving out for college or work."

### **Q11: Some people like to face challenges by themselves. Others prefer to have help from another person when facing difficult situations. Which do you prefer and why?**
1. "Facing challenges alone allows individuals to **manage challenges independently**, building confidence in their abilities."
2. "Seeking help fosters collaboration, but working solo cultivates **autonomy** and personal growth through problem-solving."
3. "Both approaches are valuable, but self-reliance enhances one’s ability to adapt and thrive under pressure."

### **Q44: Do you prefer a teacher who supervises closely or one who lets students work independently?**

2. "Close supervision can be helpful initially, but independent work enhances **decision-making skills** and problem-solving confidence."
3. "Students thrive when they are allowed to balance guidance with opportunities to **foster self-reliance**."

2. "These responsibilities **foster self-reliance**, ensuring children grow up prepared to handle daily challenges independently."
3. "Participating in chores promotes **teamwork and responsibility**, instilling a sense of contribution to the family."

### **Q52: Should young people learn domestic skills like cooking, sewing, and child care?**
1. "Learning domestic skills prepares young people for **self-sufficient living**, reducing reliance on others for basic needs."

1. "Working independently fosters **self-reliance**, as employees take responsibility for their outcomes and success."
2. "Such environments help individuals **manage challenges independently**, leading to personal and professional growth."
3. "Autonomy in the workplace cultivates **decision-making skills**, allowing employees to thrive in complex situations."

### **Q57: Some universities expect students to choose a major field of study when they enter university. Other universities allow students to wait until the second or third year before deciding on a major field of study. Which do you think is better? Why?**

2. "Early selection fosters focus, but delaying allows students to **enhance adaptability** by exploring multiple disciplines."
3. "Flexible timelines support **self-discovery** while promoting **decision-making skills** through thoughtful exploration."

2. "Optional math classes promote **self-sufficient living**, as students tailor their education to their career needs."
3. "Both approaches foster **decision-making skills**, whether by mastering critical thinking or prioritizing relevant coursework."
