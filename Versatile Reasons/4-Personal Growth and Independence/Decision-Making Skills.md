### Decision-Making Skills

### **Q11: Do you prefer facing challenges alone or with help? Why?**
1. "Facing challenges alone helps individuals **develop decision-making skills** by requiring them to independently assess problems and solutions."
2. "Seeking help allows people to **weigh options effectively**, benefiting from diverse perspectives while retaining decision-making authority."
3. "Both approaches refine judgment; working solo builds confidence in decisions, while collaboration improves **problem-solving** abilities."
