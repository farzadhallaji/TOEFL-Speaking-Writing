### **Main Files**

- **`independent.md`**
  - Contains resources and templates for Independent Writing Tasks.
- **`integrated.md`**
  - Contains resources and templates for Integrated Writing Tasks.
- **`independent-question.md`**
  - A collection of Independent Writing Task questions from **Neo Exam 1 to 113**.
- **`INDEP.MD`**
  - A brainstorming file to explore ideas for Independent Writing Tasks.
- **`qtask1.md`**
  - A comprehensive list of questions for Speaking Task 1 from **Neo Exam 1 to 113**.
- **`TASK1.MD`**
  - A brainstorming file to explore ideas for Speaking Task 1.

### **Speaking Task Files**

### **Versatile Reasons**

This directory organizes a variety of reasons and benefits categorized into specific themes. Each category includes sub-topics with detailed explanations and related collocations to support both writing and speaking tasks.

5. **Career and Academic Development**
    Topics include: Resume-building, Skill Enhancement, Time Management, and more.
6. **Environmental and Ethical Impact**
    Topics include: Sustainability, Wildlife Preservation, Ethical Considerations, and more.
7. **Health and Well-being**
    Topics include: Mental Health, Physical Fitness, Work-life Balance, and more.
8. **Practical Benefits**
    Topics include: Cost-effectiveness, Accessibility, Efficiency, and more.

## **Usage**

1. **Writing Tasks**:
   - Use `independent.md` and `integrated.md` for structured templates and practice.
   - Refer to `independent-question.md` for a variety of exam questions to simulate test scenarios.
   - Brainstorm ideas using `INDEP.MD`.
2. **Speaking Tasks**:
   - Prepare with `task1.md`, `task2.md`, `task3.md`, and `task4.md`, which provide templates and collocations.
   - Use `qtask1.md` to practice questions from exams.
3. **Versatile Reasons**:
   - Find organized benefits and reasons to support your answers in writing and speaking tasks.
