## Templates

### **Template 1: Personal Preference**

**Question:** Some people prefer [Option A]; others prefer [Option B]. Which do you prefer and why?

**Response:** I personally prefer [Option A/B] for two main reasons. Firstly, [reason 1 with explanation or example]. For instance, [personal anecdote or specific detail]. Secondly, [reason 2 with explanation or example]. For example, [specific example]. Overall, these are the two primary reasons why I prefer [Option A/B].

------

### **Template 2: Agree/Disagree Statement**

**Question:** Do you agree or disagree with the following statement: [Statement]?

**Response:** I [agree/disagree] with the statement that [restate statement]. Firstly, [reason 1 with explanation or example]. For instance, [specific example]. Secondly, [reason 2 with explanation or example]. For example, [specific example]. Therefore, I believe that [restate agreement/disagreement].

------

### **Template 3: Comparative Question**

**Question:** Some people think [Perspective A]; others think [Perspective B]. What do you think?

**Response:** In my opinion, [Perspective A/B] is better for two main reasons. First, [reason 1 with explanation or example]. For instance, [specific example]. Second, [reason 2 with explanation or example]. For example, [specific detail]. For these reasons, I think [Perspective A/B] is more suitable.

------

### **Template 4: Problem-Solution**

**Question:** Imagine a situation where [problem]. Do you think [solution] is a good idea? Why or why not?

**Response:** I [agree/disagree] with the idea of [solution] as a way to address [problem]. Firstly, [reason 1 with explanation or example]. For instance, [specific example]. Secondly, [reason 2 with explanation or example]. For example, [specific detail]. Therefore, I believe that [solution] is [effective/ineffective].

------

### **Template 5: Importance of Factors**

**Question:** Some people think [Factor A] is more important than [Factor B]. Do you agree?

**Response:** I believe that [Factor A/B] is more important for [topic]. First, [reason 1 with explanation or example]. For example, [specific example]. Second, [reason 2 with explanation or example]. For instance, [specific detail]. Therefore, I think [Factor A/B] plays a more significant role in [topic].
