### Sustainability

### **Q32: Should cities replace their only green spaces with housing complexes to address overcrowding? Why or why not?**
1. "Replacing green spaces compromises **sustainability efforts**, as these areas are essential for maintaining ecological balance."
2. "Green spaces promote **pollution reduction** by absorbing carbon dioxide and filtering pollutants."
3. "Cities should prioritize **sustainable policies**, such as vertical housing or mixed-use developments, to address overcrowding without sacrificing natural environments."

### **Q21: Some people prefer buying used books, while others only buy new ones. Which do you prefer and why?**
1. "Buying used books aligns with **eco-friendly practices**, reducing the demand for paper and preserving forests."
2. "Opting for pre-owned books supports **sustainable choices**, extending the life cycle of materials."
3. "While new books support authors directly, used books contribute to **resource conservation** and environmental health."
