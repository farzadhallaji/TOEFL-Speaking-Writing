### Adaptability and Leadership



### **Q81: Quick vs. deliberative decision-making in leadership: Which is better and why?**
1. "Quick decision-making demonstrates **decisiveness**, which is critical in high-pressure situations requiring immediate action."
2. "Deliberative approaches enable **strategic decision-making**, ensuring long-term solutions by analyzing all potential outcomes."
3. "Effective leaders balance **leadership dynamics**, applying quick decisions in crises and deliberative approaches for complex issues."

### **Q102: Close supervision vs. autonomy in management: Which is better and why?**
1. "Close supervision provides **team alignment**, ensuring that goals are met consistently and standards are maintained."
2. "Autonomy fosters **team collaboration** and innovation, as employees feel empowered to take ownership of their tasks."
3. "Situational management combines both approaches, adapting to team needs and project requirements for optimal outcomes."

### **Q91: Professors eligible for promotions/pay raises based on student evaluations?**
1. "Incorporating student feedback promotes **adaptive leadership**, as professors adjust their teaching to meet learner needs."
2. "While evaluations encourage improvement, **strategic decision-making** ensures that other performance metrics, such as research contributions, are also considered."
3. "Balanced metrics for promotions foster **situational management**, recognizing diverse contributions to academic success."
