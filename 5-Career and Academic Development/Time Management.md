### Time Management

### **Q8: Should university students have part-time jobs? Why?**
1. "Part-time jobs teach students **time management** by requiring them to balance work, studies, and personal commitments."
2. "These roles help students learn to **prioritize tasks**, ensuring they focus on both academic and professional goals."
3. "Managing schedules during part-time jobs prepares students for the multitasking demands of their future careers."

### **Q99: Should universities limit students’ work hours for academic success? Why?**
1. "Limiting work hours ensures students maintain their **academic priorities**, avoiding burnout and underperformance in studies."
2. "Restrictions help students **manage schedules** better, striking a balance between financial and educational needs."
3. "By placing limits, universities foster a culture of **efficient time management**, enabling students to focus on learning outcomes."

### **Q102: Close supervision vs. autonomy in management: Which is better and why?**
1. "Close supervision ensures employees **prioritize tasks** effectively, especially when they are new to their roles."
2. "Autonomy helps employees develop **self-discipline**, enabling them to manage schedules independently."
3. "A hybrid approach, combining **guided oversight** with freedom, ensures both efficiency and innovation in task execution."
