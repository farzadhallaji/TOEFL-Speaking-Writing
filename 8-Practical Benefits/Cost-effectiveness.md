### **Cost-effectiveness - Sample Sentences for Questions**

---

#### **Q2: Some think that natural talent and ability are more important for an athlete; others think that hard work is more important. What do you think?**
1. "Hard work is a **cost-effective solution** for athletes to achieve success, as it involves consistent effort rather than relying on inherent abilities."  
2. "Focusing on hard work helps **maximize value** in training sessions, ensuring steady improvement over time."  
3. "While natural talent may be advantageous, hard work is the more **financially practical** path, as it doesn’t rely on expensive coaching or facilities."

---

#### **Q5: Some people prefer shopping in large shopping malls; others prefer shopping in smaller shops. Which do you prefer and why?**
1. "Shopping in smaller shops can be a **budget-friendly choice**, as they often provide unique items at lower prices compared to malls."  
2. "Large malls may offer a wide variety, but smaller shops are often the more **economical option**, avoiding the **premium costs** associated with big brands."  
3. "For those aiming to **reduce expenses**, smaller shops are ideal as they offer a personalized shopping experience without overspending."

---

#### **Q50: Imagine you need housing next semester and found two options: one near campus but expensive, the other far but affordable. Which would you choose and why?**
1. "Choosing affordable housing farther away is the more **cost-effective solution**, helping students allocate funds to other important needs."  
2. "The farther apartment is a **low-cost option** that makes saving money possible, even if it requires additional commute time."  
3. "An **economical option** like affordable housing can help students **reduce expenses** and manage their budgets more effectively."

---

#### **Q25: Some people are willing to spend a lot on vacations to do something special, while others prefer a low-budget vacation. Which is better and why?**
1. "Low-budget vacations are a **budget-friendly choice**, allowing travelers to enjoy memorable experiences without financial strain."  
2. "Opting for a **low-cost option** in travel provides an authentic and enjoyable experience while staying within financial limits."  
3. "Vacations focused on saving money help travelers **maximize value**, enjoying more activities for less expense."
