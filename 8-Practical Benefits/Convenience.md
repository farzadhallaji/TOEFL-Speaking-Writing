### **Convenience - Sample Sentences for Questions**

---

#### **Q3: Do you prefer listening to recorded music or going to live music performances? Explain why.**
1. "Listening to recorded music offers **ultimate convenience**, allowing me to enjoy my favorite songs at any time without needing to attend a live event."  
2. "Recorded music is **easily accessible**, enabling me to create personalized playlists for different moods and activities."  
3. "Live music performances, though less convenient, provide a unique experience that recorded music cannot replicate."

---

#### **Q6: Some teachers prefer students to send questions about coursework and assignments by email. Other teachers prefer students ask these questions in person. Which do you think is better? Explain why.**
1. "Email is **extremely convenient** because it allows students to ask questions at their own pace and receive detailed responses."  
2. "Asking questions in person ensures **immediate access** to clarification, which might not be possible through email."  
3. "Email provides a **hassle-free solution** for communication, especially when students or teachers have conflicting schedules."

---

#### **Q10: Some people prefer to work in an office; others prefer to work from home. Which do you prefer and why?**
1. "Working from home offers the **ultimate convenience**, as it eliminates commuting time and allows for flexible scheduling."  
2. "An office provides **convenient access** to resources and fosters collaboration that might be harder to achieve remotely."  
3. "Home setups can be tailored for **one-stop convenience**, accommodating both personal and professional needs."

---

#### **Q76: Some people prefer to shop regularly at a store that is close and convenient to them, even though it might be expensive. Others prefer to shop regularly at a store with the lowest prices, even if it is far away. Which do you prefer? Use details and examples to explain your opinion.**
1. "Shopping at a nearby store offers **immediate access** to essentials, making it a practical choice for busy days."  
2. "Although a distant store may offer lower prices, a closer one saves time and effort, providing a **highly convenient** solution."  
3. "Conveniently located stores reduce transportation costs and offer a **hassle-free solution** for everyday shopping."

---

#### **Q61: Would you prefer living in an expensive apartment near campus or an affordable one farther away? Explain your choice.**
1. "Living near campus ensures **convenient access** to classes, libraries, and other facilities, saving both time and energy."  
2. "An affordable apartment farther away might be cost-effective, but it lacks the **ultimate convenience** of a short commute."  
3. "Proximity to campus provides a **hassle-free solution** for attending early morning or late-night classes."

---

#### **Q68: Do you prefer shopping in specialty shops or supermarkets? Why?**
1. "Supermarkets offer **one-stop convenience**, making it easy to buy a variety of items in a single trip."  
2. "Specialty shops might provide unique items, but supermarkets are **highly convenient** for regular and bulk shopping."  
3. "Shopping at a supermarket is a **hassle-free solution**, as it eliminates the need to visit multiple stores."
